Recognition Timeline

1989•First Guinness Recognition

Initially recognized as first and fastest circumnavigators by Guinness World Records

1991•Title Challenged

Guinness dropped "first" title after British Army achievement, keeping only "fastest"

1991•New Record Set

Completed second journey in record time, beating British Army by significant margin

1993-1998•Legal Battle Period

Five-year legal fight in Calcutta High Court for proper recognition and damages

1998•Complete Victory

Guinness officially recognized them as first and fastest, Choudhury Trophy established

Present•Lasting Legacy

Continued recognition in all Guinness World Records editions and motor rallying community

Legacy & Impact

Choudhury Trophy

The Guinness World Records Road Challenge trophy is named in their honor, recognizing their pioneering contribution to motor rallying.

Kolkata on World Map

Their achievement put Kolkata and India on the world map of motor rallying, inspiring future generations of adventurers.
